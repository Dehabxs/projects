Entities

To display a less than sign (<) we must write: (& lt;) or (& #60;) make sure they are all together when used no spaces

Symbol Entities

To add symbols use the entity name or the entity number (a decimal or a hexadecimal reference) for the symbol such as (€), (& euro;), (& #8364;), (& #x20AC;) make sure when using the () they are all in one word .
